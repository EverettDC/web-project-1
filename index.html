The Greek pantheon is made up if multiple races of deities. The oldest are the Primordials, they are beings that make up the universe, like the Earth and Sky. Then the Titans, they are much less consequential, but the original 12 are children of Gaia and Uranus who are both Primordials. Then the titans Cronos and Rhea gave birth to the first 6 Gods .
