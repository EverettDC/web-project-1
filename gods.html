Gods

The gods are the main Deities of Greek myth, there are hundreds of them, so to keep this doable I will only cover the 12-13 Olypians, which are the main gods.

Zeus

Zeus is the King of the gods, yougest of 6 born of Rhea and Cronos, he is married to his sister Hera, Queen of the gods. He is the god of lightning and the sky. Due to the many very differning accounts of Zeus and how many children he had and with who only the children who are mostly agreed upon by the masses will be included so most of his supposed children are going to be atributed to others. For example Aphrodite in one story is a daughter of Uranus but in anther story she is the daughter of Zeus and Dione.

Hestia

Hestia is the eldest of Cronos and Rhea. She the goddess of hearth(a hearth is like a big fire place) and home, she is also one of the 3 Virgin Goddesses.

Apollo

Apollo is a son of Zeus and Leto, he is not married and is the god of the sun, archery, music, dance, plauge, light, poetry, prophecies, and truth. He is the twin brother of Artemis.

Apollo's God Children

  • Aristaeus
  • Asclepius

Artemis

Artemis is the daughter of Zeus and Leto, twin sister of Apollo and one of the three Virgin Goddesses, she is the goddess of archery, the moon, the hunt, chastity, the wilderness, nature, and rather ironicaly childbirth, this is because right after she was born she helped her mother give birth to her brother Apollo.

Aphrodite

Aphrodite is the daughter of Uranus (alone), she is the goddess of love, lust, procreation, beauty, passion, and pleasure. She is married to Hepheastus, but doesn't like him and does not have any children with him.

Hermes

Hermes is the son of Maia (a Pleiad) and Zeus. He is not Married, he is the god of thieves, cunning, language, wit, messages, mechants, and athletes.

Athena

Athena is the daughter of Zeus (and techically Metis), she was born after Hepeastus split Zeus' head open with an axe and she popped out fully grown and with a spear and a full set of armour. She is the goddess of wisdom and warfare and handicraft. She is the third and final Virgin Goddess.

Dionysus

Dionysus is the son of Zeus and Semele (who is Zeus' grand daughter), Dionysus married to a formerly mortal Ariadne, they met after she was dumped by Theseus after haveing fallen for him. He is the god of wine, festivity, ritual madness(wierd choice but ok), theatre, religeous ecstacy and fertility. He does not have any god children.
