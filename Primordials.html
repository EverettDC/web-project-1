I will only be listing the Deities who I think are the most important, if I did all of them this would be as long as a novel

Primordials

Primordials are the first beings, they are the oldest and most powerful of all the Greek Deities.

Chaos

Chaos is the first ever thing in the universe, most times Chaos is described as a woman. Chaos is the Primordial Goddess of creation, she is also sometimes called 'The Void'

Chaos' Children

  • Gaia(Earth)
  • Nyx(Night)
  • Erebus(Darkness)
  • Tartarus(The Underworld)
  • Eros(Desire)

Nyx

Nyx is the Primordial Goddess of the night, sister-consort of Erebus (her brother (this happens alot)), she has children with both Erebus and herself (this also happens often). She is often refered to as Night

      • Apate(Deceit)
      • Oizys(Distress)
      • Geras(Old Age)
